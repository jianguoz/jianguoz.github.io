I am a Senior Research Scientist at Salesforce AI Research and a recipient of the 2024 Annual T&P (Technology and Product) All-Star Awards, awarded to the top 1% with approximately 150 winners across Technology. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago, where I was advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu in the BDSC Lab. My current research primarily focuses on AI Agents and Conversational AI. Specifically, my work involves:
  • Large-scale and advanced data collection, processing, synthesis, verification, and more.
  • Training large action models and designing AI agents.
  • Creating end-to-end dialogue systems, with an emphasis on accurate natural language understanding and dialogue state tracking without complicated pipelines.
